Dangers of Drinking and Driving

Drinking and Driving Kills

Over 10,000 killed per year in drinking and driving incidents

According to Bankrate.com, drunk driving causes more than 10,000 deaths per year, about 1/3 of all traffic-related deaths.

IT IS SO PREVENTABLE

There are so many different options you can take than drunk driving!

If you've been drinking, don't get in the driver's seat. Simple as that, call a cab, get a friend to drive you home, walk! Just don't drive.
